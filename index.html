Once upon a time there was a group of shapes. They were mostly black and rectangular. They were mean and very serious

And so they kicked the three shapes that did'nt conform out of their gang, which was called the right angled gang.

And so these three shapes were very sad, but found great joy and comfort being around each other. They soon started having a lot of fun, started rocking and rolling, started being silly, and become the most well-known shape gang in the Universe.

They even started making fun of the squares and rectangles in what they like to call 'Square Offs'!
